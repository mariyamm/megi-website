Who I Am ?

I am Miglena Dimitrova, a corporate and competition attorney, entrepreneur, and legal tech enthusiast with 20 years of experience helping startups, scaleups, and international businesses navigate complex legal challenges with clarity and confidence.

I studied law at the University of National and World Economy in Sofia and completed an LL.M. in European Law at the University of Hamburg. I’ve been a qualified attorney since 2005, beginning my career in leading law firms where I gained hands-on experience in M&A, corporate law, and competition law.

In 2012, I founded MDMI Legal, a firm focused on corporate and gambling law, where I led major international transactions and regulatory projects. Later, in 2020, I launched Lawminded as a legal tech startup supporting founders and early-stage companies with fast, tailored legal solutions.

I created Legal Space of Miglena Dimitrova to offer a modern, business-oriented legal service — one that combines deep regulatory insight with clarity, agility, and a global outlook.
